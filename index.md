### Jiff Journal is a new-age journal app for iPhone, iPad and Mac.<br><br>

> Save every notable experience of your life.  
> 
> Recall your memories based on text, date or feelings.  
> 
> Sync across multiple apple devices: iPhone, iPad and Mac.  
> 
> It's private: online backup stored in your private iCloud database.
